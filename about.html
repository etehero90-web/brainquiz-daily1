About the App

What is BrainQuiz Daily?

BrainQuiz Daily is a scientifically inspired memory test that helps you track your cognitive health over time. Based on principles used in clinical dementia screening, our quiz presents 5 progressively challenging memory tasks that assess your short-term memory, attention span, and cognitive flexibility.

Unlike complicated clinical tests, BrainQuiz Daily is designed to be fun, quick, and accessible for everyone — from young adults to seniors. Whether you're worried about your own memory or want to help a loved one, our test provides a simple snapshot of cognitive performance.

The app is completely free, requires no sign-up, and is available in 12 languages, making it accessible to people around the world.

How It Works

How the Test Works

1

Choose Your Language

Select from 12 available languages. The entire test — questions, answers, and results — will appear in your chosen language.

2

Memorize the Items

Each question shows you items to memorize — sequences, numbers, colors, or stories. You have a limited time to study them carefully.

3

Answer from Memory

The items disappear and you answer questions based purely on your memory. The questions get progressively harder.

4

Get Your Result

Receive a detailed result with your grade, personalized health tips, and the option to share your score with friends and family.

5

Practice Daily

Regular brain training is key to preventing cognitive decline. Try the test every day and track your progress over time.

Results

Understanding Your Score

Your result is based on the number of correct answers out of 5. Each grade comes with personalized health recommendations.

Score Grade Meaning
5 / 5 🏆 Perfect Incredible memory! Your brain is in excellent shape.
4 / 5 🥇 Excellent Great memory! Just one step away from perfect.
3 / 5 👍 Good Decent memory with room to improve. Keep practicing!
1–2 / 5 💪 Keep Going Memory improves with regular exercise. Don't give up!
0 / 5 ❤️ Seek Support Please consult a healthcare professional for evaluation.
